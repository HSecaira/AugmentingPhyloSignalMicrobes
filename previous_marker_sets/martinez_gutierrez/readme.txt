This direcotyr contains the profile Hidden Markov Models for 41 single-copy marker genes
described in "Phylogenetic Signal, Congruence, and Uncertainty across Bacteria and Archaea"
by Martinez-Gutierrez & Aylward, 2021. Molecular Biology and Evolution. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1093/molbev/msab254

FIles and directories:
	embl.hmm: pHMM of 41 SCG marker genes. Downloaded from the Supp. material of article: https://github.com/faylward/markerfinder
	MG_BitScoreCutoffs.allhits.txt: file containing the bitscore cutoff threshold for marker genes
